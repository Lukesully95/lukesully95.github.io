🎯 About Me
The Story So Far

I'm Luke Sullivan — a Global Business major with a concentration in Marketing at Fordham University Lincoln Center (Gabelli School of Business), carrying a 3.5 GPA. I have a dynamic background spanning sales, marketing, wealth management, nonprofit work, and the creative arts.

I thrive at the intersection of people and strategy — whether I'm prospecting on LinkedIn Sales Navigator, managing client relationships, or stepping in front of a camera. My experiences range from working with cutting-edge AI at Harvey to supporting philanthropic missions at the Christopher Reeve Foundation.

I'm CRM certified, deeply curious about real estate, and constantly exploring how AI tools can streamline workflows. At Fordham, I participated in the Ground Floor entrepreneurship project and took on a consulting challenge devising growth strategies for McCormick & Company.

Outside of work, I've modeled at Bergdorf Goodman, appeared in an Apple commercial, a Liberty Bank commercial, and did background work for Life and Beth. I bring the same energy, discipline, and creativity to every opportunity I pursue.
